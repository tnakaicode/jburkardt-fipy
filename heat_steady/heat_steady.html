heat_steady
Solution of the Steady Heat Equation

heat_steady, FENICS scripts which solve versions of the steady heat equation on a 2D domain.

Source Code:

heat_steady_01 solves the steady heat equation over a rectangle with constant thermal diffusivity.

  • heat_steady_01.py, the FENICS script.
  • heat_steady_01.txt, the output file.
  • heat_steady_01.png, a plot of the solution.

heat_steady_02 solves the steady heat equation over a square with constant thermal diffusivity, and a point source.

  • heat_steady_02.py, the FENICS script.
  • heat_steady_02.txt, the output file.
  • heat_steady_02.png, a plot of the solution.

heat_steady_03 solves the steady heat equation over a square with a smoothly-varying thermal diffusivity.

  • heat_steady_03.py, the FENICS script.
  • heat_steady_03.txt, the output file.
  • heat_steady_03.png, a plot of the solution.

heat_steady_04 solves the steady heat equation over a rectangle with a piecewise constant thermal diffusivity.

  • heat_steady_04.py, the FENICS script.
  • heat_steady_04.txt, the output file.
  • heat_steady_04.png, a plot of the solution.
