GLACIER
Steady 2D Stokes Model of a Glacier

GLACIER is a FENICS script which defines the steady 2D Stokes equations for a model of the motion of ice in a glacier, by William Mitchell.

Since this example was published, FENICS has changed, and several commands used in the example no longer work. This seems to happen A LOT.

Reference:

  1. William Mitchell,
    Exact and numerical solutions for Stokes flows in glaciers,
    Master's thesis,
    Department of Mathematics and Statistics,
    University of Alaska, Fairbanks, Alasaka,
    August 2012.
